Fire Force

Fire Force 「炎炎ノ消防隊 , Enen no Shōbōtai」 is a manga series created by Atsushi Okubo. The series began publication on September 23, 2015 in Weekly Shonen Magazine.

A 24-episode anime series adaptation aired between July and December 2019. The second season of the anime began airing from July 3, 2020.

Human Combustion, an inexplicable phenomenon in which people mysteriously burst into flames, plagues mankind. Shinra Kusakabe, a boy who wishes to become a hero, joins the Eighth Fire Squad in order to save humanity and to uncover the mystery behind his own past and his power.

Main characters

Maki Oze

Maki Oze (茉希尾瀬, Maki Oze) is the daughter of General Oze and a Second Generation pyrokinetic serving as Unit Leader of Tokyo's Special Fire Force Company 8.

Maki is a pretty young adult of above-average height and fair complexion. She is slender with a curvaceous and well-endowed figure. At the same time, she is athletically built with well-defined muscle.

Akitaru Ōbi

Akitaru Ōbi (秋樽桜備, Akitaru Ōbi) is the captain of Tokyo's Special Fire Force Company 8. Akitaru is a well-built young man with short, somewhat unruly dark hair, with the sides of his head being shaven. Akitaru also has small dark brown eyes. He is seen wearing bunker gear, which consists of a black tank top, buckled at the waist with a belt, which presses a middle part that's strapped to his shoulders, and dark pants.

Shinra Kusakabe

Shinra Kusakabe (日くさ下か部べ 森しん羅ら, Kusakabe Shinra) is a Third and Fourth Generation pyrokinetic in the service of Special Fire Force Company 8. After his mother's death, and supposedly his younger brother's as well, Shinra joined the Special Fire Force to achieve his goal of becoming a hero, saving people from Spontaneous Human Combustion, and uncovering the truth behind his family's death twelve years ago.
